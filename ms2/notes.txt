1.) Download VNC viewer for your OS: 
https://www.realvnc.com/en/connect/download/viewer/ 

2.) Once the Raspberry pi is powered on, search for a wifi access point named either IOT-MIS-* or MPC* and 
connect to it 

3.) Find the password for your Raspberry Pi’s network in the inventory sheet: 
https://bit.ly/checkoutIoT 

4.) Once you’re connected, start VNC viewer and enter the ip address 192.168.4.1 

5.) At the connection prompt, the name of the device is ‘pi’ and the password is ‘raspberry’ 

6.) Now the connection between the pi and your computer is established at the cost of internet to your computer.
